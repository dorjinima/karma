Experience Bhutanese Culture & Cuisine

Bhutanese Cuisine

A Fiery Himalayan Delight

Bhutanese food is simple yet bold, featuring rice, chilies, cheese, and fresh vegetables. Savor these iconic dishes during your Bhutan tour:

  • Ema Datshi (Chili & Cheese Stew)
  • Phaksha Paa (Pork with Red Chilies)
  • Jasha Maru (Spicy Chicken Curry)
  • Red Rice
  • Suja (Butter Tea)
  • Ara (Local Alcohol)

Bhutanese Culture

Rooted in Happiness & Tradition

Bhutan measures success through Gross National Happiness (GNH), reflecting its deep ties to spirituality, nature, and community.

  • Tsechu Festivals: Vibrant masked dances
  • Monasteries & Dzongs: Spiritual landmarks
  • Traditional Dress: Gho and Kira
  • Art & Handicrafts: Intricate designs
  • Community Living: Harmonious lifestyle
